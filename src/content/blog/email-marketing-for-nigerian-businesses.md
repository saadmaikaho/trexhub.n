Email marketing has an average ROI of 4,200% globally — that's ₦42 back for every ₦1 spent. Yet most Nigerian businesses completely ignore it. Here's why it should be a core part of your marketing strategy.

## The Problem With Relying Only on Social Media

When you build your audience on Instagram or Facebook, you're building on rented land. The platform can change its algorithm, restrict your reach, or suspend your account at any time. Your email list and WhatsApp contacts? Those are yours. Nobody can take them away.

## What Makes Email & WhatsApp Marketing So Powerful

### 1. Direct Access to Your Audience

When you send an email or WhatsApp message, it lands directly in your contact's inbox — no algorithm deciding whether to show it or not. Email open rates average 20–30%. WhatsApp open rates? Over 90%.

### 2. Automation That Works While You Sleep

Set up a sequence once, and it runs forever:

- Someone signs up for your lead magnet → they get a welcome email
- 3 days later → they get a case study showing your results
- Day 7 → they get a special offer
- Day 14 → a follow-up if they haven't responded

This is how businesses generate consistent revenue without constantly chasing new leads.

### 3. Segmentation = Personalisation at Scale

Not all your contacts are the same. Email lets you segment by:

- Where they are in the buying journey
- What service they're interested in
- Whether they've bought from you before

A real estate lead gets different emails than an e-commerce brand. Personalised emails get 6x higher transaction rates.

## Getting Started: Build Your List

You need a reason for people to give you their email or WhatsApp number. Common lead magnets that work in Nigeria:

- A free guide ("5 Ways to Get More Customers Online")
- A free consultation or audit
- A discount or special offer
- A webinar or training

## WhatsApp Marketing in Nigeria

WhatsApp is particularly powerful in Nigeria because it's the primary communication app for most Nigerians. With WhatsApp Business API, you can:

- Send broadcast messages to your entire contact list
- Set up automated responses and follow-up sequences
- Qualify leads through conversational flows
- Send reminders, updates, and offers
